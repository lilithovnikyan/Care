AccessOnTime takes great pride in coordinating the logistics to and from healthcare visits for your injured worker, saving you time and money. From routine visits to challenging multi-step appointments, we can transport your injured worker from home or work to a doctor’s office, hospital or rehabilitation facility anywhere in the U.S.

Using an unrivaled quality assurance methodology – Points of Touch – we closely supervise all trips to make sure your injured worker arrives at their destination right on schedule. If a driver encounters traffic or an injured worker is delayed, we are notified and respond with an appropriate course of action. This proactive approach facilitates the injured worker’s compliance with their medical care plan, translating to a faster recovery and an expedited return to work.
